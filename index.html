A little about me

I am a streaming miniature painter who evolved from acrylic paints to the amazing but older oil paints.
While going through this evolution I have gained exclusive skills and knowledge that I want to share with others.
With the use of twitch, youtube, instagram and this personal website I hope to share with others the wonderfull
world of oil paints and how they are very effective at reducing your pile of shame to nothing. Here you will be able to
take a glance at my galleries both Warhammer 40K and Warhammer AOS. You will also find links to all of my social media
platforms to make it easy to find my content in a single location.
